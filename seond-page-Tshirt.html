T shirt origin: The T-shirt started as an item of underwear - in the 19th Century the t-shirt was born when labourers in America cut their jumpsuits in half to cope with the summer heat. Although they were starting to be manufactured in1898, the T-shirt really made it in 1913 when it became standard issue in the US Navy's uniform.

The sleeveless t-shirt, or tank top, evolved from undershirts and was popularized as part of bathing suits in the early 20th century. Initially, these tops were more like athletic wear, later becoming mainstream fashion.

The V-neck, a V-shaped neckline, has a rich history, evolving from a practical undershirt design to a fashionable statement piece. Originally, V-necks were favored for their ability to conceal the collar of inner garments when layered under other shirts, according to Wikipedia. They gained popularity in the 1960s as a variation on the traditional crew-neck t-shirt.
