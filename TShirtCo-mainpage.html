If you were curious on how a Tshirt is made here are a couple steps you can follow to produce one. Prepare your workspace and materials: Gather your fabric, pattern, sewing machine, and other necessary tools. Pin and cut the pattern: Pin the pattern onto your fabric and cut out the pieces. Sew the seams: Sew the shoulder seams, neckline, and sleeve seams. Add sleeves and hem: Sew the sleeves to the armholes and hem the bottom and sleeves.

You'll need to pre-wash it, prepare a dye bath (using either commercial or homemade dyes), and then immerse the fabric, stir, rinse, and wash it again. Choose a fabric that is suitable for dyeing, and avoid items like 100% polyester or items with special finishes.
